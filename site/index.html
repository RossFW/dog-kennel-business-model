What is CAGR? (The Math)

CAGR = Compound Annual Growth Rate. It answers: "If something grew from $X to $Y over N years, what consistent annual % rate does that represent?"

Formula: CAGR = (End Value ÷ Start Value)1/Years − 1

Applied: ($4.30B ÷ $1.85B)1/10 − 1 = (2.324)0.10 − 1 = 8.6%

Each year's growth builds on the previous year's larger base — like compound interest. The market more than doubles in 10 years. Compare to the S&P 500's long-run ~10% CAGR — this market nearly keeps pace.

Smart Play: Run Both. Boarding dogs participate in daycare during the day. The space earns daycare revenue during day hours AND boarding revenue overnight. This is how Dogtopia, Barking Hound Village, and most premium operators run. The 14,612 SF facility is well-suited for this.

Typical Daily Schedule

6:30 AM Facility prep: clean play areas, fill water stations, check safety 1–2 staff
7:00 AM Doors open — morning drop-off rush; check-in each dog (vaccination check, intake) 2–3 staff
9:30 AM Active play supervision; rotate playgroups by size & temperament 2–3 staff
12:00 PM Mandatory rest period / midday nap time (dogs need downtime — prevents overstimulation) 1–2 staff
1:00 PM Afternoon play; grooming appointments happen in this window 2–3 staff
4:30 PM Evening pick-up rush — peak staffing needed; checkout each dog 2–3 staff
7:00 PM Doors close; final cleaning, facility lockdown; boarding dogs settled for night 1–2 staff
Overnight Boarding monitoring — camera system with on-call staff, OR 1 overnight attendant on-site 1 on-call

Regulations & Licensing

Three layers of compliance: State (Georgia) → County (DeKalb) → City (Chamblee). You need all three.

!
Most Critical Unresolved Question: Does VC (Village Commercial) zoning in Chamblee permit a dog kennel/boarding facility by right, or does it require a Special Use Permit (SUP)? Call Chamblee Planning & Development first: (770) 986-5010.

Licensing Path — Rough Sequence

1

Confirm VC Zoning Allows Kennel Use

Call Chamblee Planning & Development: (770) 986-5010. Is kennel/daycare a permitted use by right, or does it require a Special Use Permit (SUP)? An SUP can take 30–90 days and involves a public hearing.

2

Apply for SUP (If Required)

File SUP application with City of Chamblee. Public notice, planning commission review, and city council approval. Timeline: 30–90 days. Hire a local land use attorney if needed.

3

Pull Building Permits (City of Chamblee)

After architectural drawings are complete. Permits needed: Building, Mechanical (HVAC), Plumbing (drains), Electrical, Fire. Budget 2–4 weeks for permit review.

4

Complete Buildout

Estimated 3–6 months of construction. Floor drains + HVAC are the critical path items.

5

Fire Marshal + Building Inspection

After buildout completes. Fire marshal inspects egress, sprinklers, occupancy for animal count. Building inspector signs off on all permitted work.

6

Certificate of Occupancy (CO)

Issued by City of Chamblee after passing all inspections. Required before opening.

7

Georgia Dept. of Agriculture — Kennel License

Apply online at agr.georgia.gov/kennel-licenses. GDA inspector does a pre-inspection. Cannot open until pre-inspection is passed. Annual renewal ($50–$400/yr based on capacity). Contact: (404) 656-4914.

8

DeKalb County Business License

Occupation Tax Certificate from DeKalb County Finance Department. Can be applied for concurrently with steps 6–7.

9

Open for Business

Realistic timeline from decision to open: 8–12 months (assuming no major zoning roadblocks).
