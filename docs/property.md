## Ownership — Family Asset (Key Advantage)

**The property is already owned by the family (father is current owner).** This fundamentally changes the business case:

- No mortgage payment required
- No rent required
- The carrying cost is **~$40,000/year in property taxes** (confirmed)
- This is effectively a sunk cost the family pays regardless of what happens with the building
- Any revenue-generating use of the property offsets this cost and then some

**Property tax back-calculation:**
- Confirmed annual tax: ~$40,000
- In Georgia, property is assessed at 40% of fair market value (FMV)
- DeKalb County + City of Chamblee total millage: ~39-42 mills (county general + school + city)
- Implied assessed value: $40,000 ÷ 0.040 ≈ **$1.0M assessed** → implying ~$2.5M FMV per county's records
- This is below the $3.5M listing ask — county appraisal vs. market price gap is normal

## Location Risks / Open Questions

- **Backs to MARTA tracks**: Noise issue for dogs? Need to assess. May also limit outdoor space layout.
- **VC zoning**: "Supports most Medical and Retail uses" — need to confirm dog kennel/boarding is specifically permitted or requires special use permit (SUP). **This is the critical zoning question to answer.** (See [docs/regulations.md](regulations.md))
- **Slab construction (1974)**: A dog facility needs floor drains everywhere. Cutting drains into a 50-year-old masonry slab is expensive and complex. This is a major buildout cost driver.
- **Dilapidated condition**: Unknown extent of needed repairs, HVAC condition, electrical, plumbing. Need full inspection.
