## Competitor Matrix

- **Services:** Daycare, overnight boarding, webcams, grooming add-ons

## White Space / Opportunities Observed

1. **Sunday hours gap**: DogPhoria (closest competitor) only open Sun 4–6pm. Commuters traveling Sunday need care.
2. **Chamblee has no premium operator**: DogPhoria is good but small. Barking Hound Village has 7 locations but none in Chamblee/north DeKalb.
3. **Membership model not dominant locally**: Dogtopia does it well (franchise); a local operator with a membership-first model could build recurring revenue and customer lock-in.
4. **14,612 SF is large**: Most competitors are in ~4,000-8,000 SF retail strips. A purpose-built, large-format facility (indoor + outdoor) could be a category differentiator.
5. **Training is high-margin**: $1,500-$2,500 per dog for board & train. If you have the space (and we do), this is worth serious consideration.
6. **MARTA proximity**: No competitor is marketing to transit-using dog owners. Doraville/Chamblee MARTA stations are walkable or bikeable to our property — niche but real.
